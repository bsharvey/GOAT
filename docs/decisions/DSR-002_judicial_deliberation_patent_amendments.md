## Synthesis Decision

Three amendments drafted and filed to the Patents directory, adding 12 new claims:

1. **Amendment 1: Divergence Key Safeguard** (Claims A1-A3) — Detects when an archetype's virtue crosses into its corresponding vice during deliberation. Includes divergence profiling, real-time scoring, intervention mechanisms (flagging, counterbalancing, re-prompting, quarantine), and cross-archetype correlated drift detection.

2. **Amendment 2: Silence Detection Mechanism** (Claims B1-B4) — Identifies evidence available but cited by no archetype. Classifies silence as benign omission, oversight, contradicting silence, or perspective gap. Mandatory pre-judgment silence check prevents decisions that ignore contradicting evidence.

3. **Amendment 3: Constitutional Layer** (Claims C1-C5) — Adds a structured, versioned, priority-hierarchical body of governing principles. The Judge must validate all decisions against mandatory principles before issuance. Constitutional compliance becomes a reward dimension in RLAF training.
