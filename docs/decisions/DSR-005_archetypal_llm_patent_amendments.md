## Question

Should the three amendments drafted for the Judicial Deliberation patent (Divergence Key Safeguard, Silence Detection, Constitutional Layer) be adapted and filed as parallel amendments for the companion Archetypal LLM patent?

## Background

DSR-002 resulted in 3 amendments with 12 new claims for the Judicial Deliberation patent. The Mirrorwalker identified during council deliberation that these innovations apply equally to the Archetypal LLM patent:

- **Divergence Key Safeguard** → applies to the council simulation mode (Claim 4 of Archetypal LLM patent)
- **Silence Detection** → applies to the Decision DNA Platform's reviewer workflow
- **Constitutional Layer** → applies to the governance of multi-archetype deliberation
